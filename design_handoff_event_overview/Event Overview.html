About

How the season runs

Season III runs over eight weeks of open-ladder play. Anyone on the roster can challenge anyone within range; results are recorded by a scorekeeper and your Elo updates the moment a match is confirmed.

The top 16 competitors at the close of the regular season advance to a single-elimination finals bracket. Inactivity decay applies after 72 hours without a recorded match, so stay active to hold your seed.
